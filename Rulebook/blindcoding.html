Blind Coding Challenge

Welcome to the most exciting and brain-twisting coding contest of Anuttaram 25. In this challenge, your logical thinking and coding memory will be tested like never before — no screen, no shortcuts.

Official Rule Book

  1. Each participant will receive coding problems to solve within a strict time frame.
  2. The twist: your screen will be hidden while typing. You must rely solely on logic, syntax memory, and debugging skills.
  3. External resources such as internet, books, or mobile phones are strictly prohibited.
  4. Solutions will be evaluated based on:
    • Correctness of the output
    • Efficiency of the algorithm
    • Clarity of logic and approach
  5. The contest consists of 2 rounds:
    • Round 1: Basic problem-solving (warm-up)
    • Round 2: Advanced coding challenge under pressure
  6. Judges’ decision will be final and binding.
  7. Any unethical practices like plagiarism or malpractice will result in immediate disqualification.

Why Blind Coding?

Blind coding isn’t just about typing without looking. It’s about sharp logic, accuracy, and presence of mind. This event ensures only the best problem solvers shine through.
