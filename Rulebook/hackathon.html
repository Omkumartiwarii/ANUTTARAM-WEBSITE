Hackathon 2025

Gear up for the ultimate innovation marathon of Anuttaram 25. Teams will collaborate to build groundbreaking solutions under pressure — creativity, teamwork, and coding speed are key.

Official Hackathon Rule Book

  1. Teams of 2-4 participants will be allowed.
  2. The hackathon duration is 24 hours. All work must be completed within this timeframe.
  3. Participants can use any technology stack, but pre-existing projects are not allowed.
  4. Solutions will be judged on:
    • Innovation and creativity
    • Technical feasibility
    • Design and user experience
    • Presentation and demo
  5. Prizes will be awarded for:
    • Best Innovation
    • Best Technical Implementation
    • Best Design & UX
  6. Any unethical practices or plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
  7. Judges’ decision will be final and binding.

Why Participate?

Hackathons challenge your problem-solving, collaboration, and speed. It's a platform to showcase talent, network with peers, and create innovative solutions in a competitive environment.
