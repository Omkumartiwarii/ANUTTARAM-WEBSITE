Quiz Championship 2025

Test your knowledge and quick thinking at Anuttaram 25. Teams will answer intriguing questions under time pressure — speed, accuracy, and teamwork will determine the winners.

Official Quiz Rule Book

  1. Teams of 2-4 participants will compete.
  2. The quiz consists of multiple rounds including:
    • Rapid Fire Round: Quick questions under time limit
    • Multiple Choice Round: Pick the correct option
    • Buzzer Round: Fastest team answers first
  3. Use of any external resources is strictly prohibited.
  4. Evaluation criteria include:
    • Correctness of answers
    • Speed and responsiveness
    • Team coordination
  5. Judges’ decision will be final and binding.
  6. Any unethical practices will result in disqualification.

Why Participate?

The Quiz event sharpens your knowledge, reflexes, and teamwork. A perfect stage to challenge yourself and compete with the brightest minds!
