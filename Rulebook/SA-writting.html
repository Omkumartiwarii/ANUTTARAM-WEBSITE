SA Writing Competition 2025

Showcase your creativity and writing skills at Anuttaram 25. Participants will craft compelling essays, stories, or short answers under time constraints — clarity, imagination, and expression are key.

Official SA Writing Rule Book

  1. Each participant will submit one piece of writing per round.
  2. Writing topics will be provided at the start of each round. Participants have 45-60 minutes to complete.
  3. Entries should be original. Use of pre-written content is strictly prohibited.
  4. Evaluation criteria include:
    • Creativity and originality
    • Clarity of expression
    • Grammar and language usage
    • Adherence to topic and structure
  5. Rounds may include:
    • Short Answer Round: Quick thought responses
    • Essay/Story Round: Detailed creative writing
  6. Judges’ decision will be final and binding.
  7. Any unethical practices will result in disqualification.

Why Participate?

SA Writing hones your creativity, language skills, and expression. It's an opportunity to share ideas, impress judges, and develop your writing talent in a competitive setting.
