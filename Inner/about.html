About Anuttaram

🌟 Welcome to Anuttaram, the annual cultural fest of Government Engineering College Sheohar. A vibrant platform celebrating music, dance, drama, arts, literature, and creativity. Join us this year and be a part of unforgettable moments filled with art, entertainment, and inspiration!

✨ What's New in 2025

🌟 Star Night Concert

Experience live music with celebrity artists and bands.

🎭 Expanded Cultural Events

More competitions in dance, drama, and fine arts with prizes.

🎨 Workshops & Exhibitions

Interactive exhibitions and creative workshops for everyone.

🏫 About the Institute

Government Engineering College Sheohar encourages excellence in academics and cultural activities, fostering holistic student development.

🔥 Fest Focus

Anuttaram 2025 brings cultural performances, art showcases, literature events, fashion shows, and celebrity nights. There’s something for everyone!
