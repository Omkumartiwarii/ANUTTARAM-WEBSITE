üè´ About the Institute

Government Engineering College Sheohar Government Engineering College (GEC) Sheohar is a prominent public technical institution located in Sheohar district, Bihar. Established in 2019 under the Department of Science and Technology, Government of Bihar, the college aims to provide quality engineering education to the youth of the region. It is affiliated with Bihar Engineering University, Patna, and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
